Notes

Note that the data on these maps represents total eviction filings, not total evictions. While the data on eviction filings represented here hopes to present a representative picture of evictions occuring in Cleveland, it almost certainly underrepresents the full universe of evictions occuring. Research has shown that formal eviction filings make up roughly a third of total evictions, as many take place outside of a courthouse.


Sources

Eviction court filings were scraped from Cleveland Municipal Court filings. Population and demographic data is from the 2019 U.S. Census Bureau ACS-5.
